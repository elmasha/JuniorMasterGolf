The Junior Master Golf Series Tournament

We are excited to invite you to participate in our Classic Edition of the Junior Master Golf Series Tournament.

Join us at Thika Sports Club for the Classic Edition of the Junior Master Golf Series Tournament to be held on 24th October, 2021 from 7am to 5pm.

The Junior Master Golf Series Tournament is an annual event that brings junior players together from all over Kenya and the region to compete for prizes and bragging rights as they grow their game of golf to the next level.

This year’s event will have the added benefit of being in support of the celebration of 100 years that Thika Sports Club has been in existence.

During this period Thika Sports Club has clearly identified itself as the home of junior golf by growing the number of juniors from about 10 juniors three years ago to 100 juniors in 2021.

For more information on TSC please visit www.thikasportsclub.co.ke

The Tournament will also act as a qualifying tournament for the Safari Junior Tour (SJT) which is a newly set up junior golf tour that is focused on growing junior golf in Kenya and the region.

For more information on SJT please visit [. ].

Our target is to host 200 juniors during the event which will also act as a qualifying tournament for the Safari Junior Tour (SJT). For more information on SJT please visit [. ].

Other games like junior soccer, lawn tennis, table tennis, squash, basketball and volleyball will also be in offer for juniors who will not be participating in the golf tournament.

There will be lots of food and drinks for the participating juniors and an award ceremony will be held when play is completed.
